Scrambled Eggs With Cheese: A Healthy Breakfast

Description:

Scrambled eggs by themselves are already pretty good. They are high in protein and taste great but a great change you can make to them is adding shredded cheese. This makes the eggs have a "gooey"er structure while also tasting even better. I have made these many many times and I would highly recommend!

    Ingredients:
  • Eggs (3-5)
  • Butter
  • Shredded moz. (idk how to spell it lol) cheese
  • Black pepper
    How to Make:
  1. Break eggs in a bowl
  2. Put a lot of black pepper
  3. Mix/Stir really well
  4. Turn up heat and put butter in pan
  5. When butter melt, pour egg mix into pan
  6. Immediatly put a bunch of cheese on egg mix
  7. Stir until it no longer looks disgusting
  8. Eat (this one is actually healthy unlike mac kraft)
